Please complete and sign this registration form and submit to the Ciena Training Coordinator at techtng@ciena.com. All registrations are considered pending until proof of payment is received and the Training coordinator has sent a confirmation to the student. Important: Student registration requests must be tied to a Purchase Order (PO) or a PO number. POs should be submitted separately via fax at 410-694-3199 or via e-mail at podistribution@ciena.com and must reference student name(s), course title, and date (if applicable). Multiple student course fees may be submitted on a single PO; however, a separate registration form must be submitted for each student. Enrollments will be confirmed via e-mail after a purchase order is received. For Instructor-led Training and Virtual Instructor-led Training, enrollment confirmation, directions, and information on local accommodations will be sent at time of registration. For Web-based training, enrollment confirmation and instructions will be sent to each individual as soon as the payment and request form are processed.

Cancellation Policy for Instructor-led and Virtual Instructor-led Training

  • Cancellations must be received in writing (email accepted) by Ciena 10 business days or more prior to the course start date in order to avoid a penalty for aggregate costs incurred by Ciena.
    • Cancellations received by Ciena within 6 to 9 business days of the course start date are subject to a cancellation fee equal to 50% of the price of the relevant training program.
    • Cancellations received by Ciena within 5 business days of the course start date are subject to a cancellation fee equal to 100% of the price of the relevant training program.
    • "No-shows" will be charged 100% of the paid per-seat price for any person not attending.
  • Substitutions are permitted up to the start of the course if the purchase order is re-applied to the new student.
  • Ciena reserves the right to reschedule courses with less than 6 students enrolled by the published registration deadline.

Cancellation Policy for Web-based Training

  • Registrations are considered active once student is notified that Ciena has provided courseware access with instructions.
  • Students will have access to the courseware for a period of 60 days from active registration unless otherwise specified in the governing customer contract.
